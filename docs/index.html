Ruthe Foushee

(ˈɹu.θi  fu.ˈʃeɪ)

[Image description: A person with chartreuse hair grins.]

Assistant Professor in the Department of Psychology, Director of the Center for Human Language & Development at The New School for Social Research, and faculty for the Developing Child Certificate Program at University of California, Berkeley.

Formerly: postdoctoral scholar in the Goldin-Meadow Laboratory at the University of Chicago; member of the Language and Cognitive Development and Berkeley Early Learning Labs at the University of California, Berkeley; impassioned Linguistics undergraduate at Harvard.
